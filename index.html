Anonymous Sudan

Anonymous Sudan claims to be a pro-Islamist hacktivist group fighting against the injustices being carried out over Muslims across the world. Citing incidents of misbehavior against the Islamic culture, they have attacked several countries in retaliation. Deeper analysis has revealed that the group appears to be a Russian front for targetting NATO members to cause disruption in NATO and and create complications in Sweden's admission to NATO. Following up with Anonymous Sudan, several hacker groups from neighboring countries with Muslim population have joined forces with them to conduct co-ordinated attacks on Indian websites.
